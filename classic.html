Classic Milk Tea

A traditional boba drink made with black tea, milk, and chewy tapioca pearls. Perfect for any time of day! The rich flavor of the tea blends smoothly with creamy milk, while the tapioca pearls add a fun, chewy texture to every sip. Enjoy it served cold over ice or warm for a cozy treat. Classic milk tea is a favorite for both newcomers and longtime boba lovers alike.
