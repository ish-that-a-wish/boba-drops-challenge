Matcha Latte

A creamy and earthy drink made with matcha green tea, milk, and chewy boba pearls. Matcha latte offers a unique blend of rich, grassy flavors from the matcha, complemented by the creaminess of milk. The chewy boba pearls add a delightful texture, making it a satisfying drink. Enjoy it chilled over ice for a refreshing treat or warm for a comforting experience.
