Taro Milk Tea

A sweet and creamy drink made with taro root, milk, and chewy boba pearls. Taro milk tea is known for its unique purple color and subtle nutty flavor. The creamy texture of the milk blends perfectly with the earthy sweetness of taro, making it a delightful treat. Enjoy it chilled over ice for a refreshing experience or warm for a comforting drink.
